Overview

Bram Bosch started his education as a bio-engineer in his home-town Nijmegen. In this period he had internships at both the Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour, and the Radboudumc. At the latter he specialized in making brain mapping and analysis accesible for a broad audience. This resulted in a plug & play method called FriendlyClearMap, which was published in May 2023. After him obtaining his Bachelor's degree, he focused on the AI aspect in Computer Science. He finished this degree with a thesis focused on automated graph mapping. Following this, he started his PhD at the Regenerative Medicine Center Utrecht, specifically at the AI Lab for Living Technologies. Some example projects of his include label free prediction and VAE's in latent spaces.
